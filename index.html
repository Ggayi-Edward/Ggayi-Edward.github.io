Features

Digitalize agreements and records by enabling the creation and signing of legally binding lease agreements electronically.

Integrate online mobile rent collection/payment and payment tracking.

Allow tenants to submit and track maintenance requests electronically.

Support geo-targeted landlord and tenant matching which will connect landlords and tenants based on preferences and budget.

Allow landlords to list detailed descriptions and photos of their properties.

Include SMS integration which will keep users informed about rent due dates, maintenance updates, and other important announcements.

Have a variety of languages to choose from while using the app.

Provide access to a curated library of Ugandan property management regulations and FAQs.
